Smoked Brisket

  • 12-14lb Beef Brisket
  • Seasoning Rub
  • Wood Pellets
  1. Trim Excess fat from Brisket
  2. Season to your desiered taste and let sit in fridge for at least 30 minutes up to 24 hours
  3. Add wood pellets to pellet smoker
  4. Set the smoker to 250 degrees fahrenheit
  5. Put Brisket on smoker until internal temperature reaches about 160 degrees
  6. Then pull off grill and wrap in butcher paper or aluminum foil
  7. Continue cooking at 250 degrees until internal temperature reaches 195-205
  8. Or until a incredibly tender
  9. Pull off the smoker and let rest in a warm cooler for at least 1 hour but no more than 8 hours
  10. Slice against the grain and Enjoy!
