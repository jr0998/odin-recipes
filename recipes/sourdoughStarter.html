Sourdough Starter

  • Whole Wheat Flour
  • Water
  1. Mix equal parts water and flour together
  2. Leave in a warm place covered for 48 hours
  3. mix equal parts water and flour and add one part of the old flour mixture(Starter)
  4. should be an equal ratio of water to flour to starter 1:1:1
  5. Leave covered in a warm place for 24 hours
  6. Repeat step 3
  7. let rest for 12 hours
  8. repeat set 3
  9. continue to feed twice a day gradually increseing the ratio of starter to four to water to 1:2:2 respectively
  10. and then eventually increase the ratio to 1:3:3
  11. After about 2 weeks the ratio should be up to 1:3:3 and the starter should be ready to cook with
